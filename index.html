A little bit about me...

Growing up in the ninties, the internet was in its infancy during my childhood. Shortly after getting a dial up connection, my friends and I discovered angelfire.com and taught ourselves primitive HTML. I never went further because I always assumed HTML was the only type of language that didn't require an advanced CS degree. I always heard that math was interval to understanding Computer Science and 8th Grade Joey hated algebra, so my coding expirence was ended.
